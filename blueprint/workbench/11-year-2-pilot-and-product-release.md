# M11 — Year-2 Pilot and Product Release

**Goal:** validate subsystem-to-product learning, custom-PCB operations, workload, program competence gates G5 and G6, and readiness for advanced systems.

**Indicative duration:** one academic year plus correction window  
**Primary owners:** Year-2 lead, manufacturing/lab lead, external reviewers  
**Depends on:** M09–M10  
**Authoring entry:** M09 and M10 pilot-ready cohort baselines pass their release gates\
**Release gate:** S3/S4 delivery, correction/re-pilot, local production reproduction, and stable-release approval are complete\
**Exit unlocks:** dependable S5/S6 assumptions

## Epic E1 — Pilot readiness and manufacturing control

- [ ] **M11-E1-T01 [REV]** Audit every S3/S4 §13.1 course manifest and unit DoD, EN/FR/accessibility/offline facet, active-learning delivery plan, component/board lead time, substitute, calibration, tool version, assessor, one-term-early staff pilot, and safety approval.
- [ ] **M11-E1-T02 [HW]** Reserve fabrication slots, panelization/reorder contingency, rework capacity, spare boards, and delayed-import simulation path.
- [ ] **M11-E1-T03 [TEST]** Dry-run G5/G6, Project M3/M4 design reviews and acceptance tests, staff PCB, firmware build, calibrated fixture/instruments, traceability, out-of-tolerance handling, and uncertainty impact.
- [ ] **M11-E1-T04 [SPEC]** Freeze hardware/software/content cohort release and board change-control dates.

## Epic E2 — Deliver and collect evidence

- [ ] **M11-E2-T01 [TEST]** Sample analog, HDL, metrology, bare-metal, control, buses, power, layout, assembly, and bring-up evidence.
- [ ] **M11-E2-T02 [TEST]** Track PCB batch size, observed first-pass fraction, defect categories, rework, part substitutions, cost, procurement delay, tool failures, and recovery time; infer no generalized yield or capability beyond justified data.
- [ ] **M11-E2-T03 [TEST]** Observe whether students use hypotheses, datasheets, test points, debug probes, and interface evidence rather than replacement guessing.
- [ ] **M11-E2-T04 [TEST]** Audit individual participation through practicals, repository history, role rotation, and defenses.
- [ ] **M11-E2-T05 [TEST]** Collect user/technician reproduction and service evidence for M4.
- [ ] **M11-E2-T06 [TEST]** Have an independent local team/technician execute the small-batch assembly, production test, serialization, nonconformance/rework, and service process; track sample size, local/imported contribution, observed first-pass/defect/rework results, cost, ambiguity, and capability gaps.
- [ ] **M11-E2-T07 [TEST]** Record G5/G6 per learner: pass, remediation/reassessment in progress, or progression hold; aggregate samples moderate the system and never infer an individual's competence.

## Epic E3 — Review, correction, and release

- [ ] **M11-E3-T01 [REV]** Conduct external analog, embedded, FPGA/HDL, PCB/manufacturing, bilingual, and assessment sampling.
- [ ] **M11-E3-T02 [REV]** Analyze prerequisite, workload, language, equipment-path, and product-quality evidence.
- [ ] **M11-E3-T03 [MAT]** Correct resources, expected ranges, board files, fixtures, rubrics, and professor notes in both languages.
- [ ] **M11-E3-T04 [TEST]** Re-pilot changed board/power/safety/firmware work and reassess unmet G5/G6 competence.
- [ ] **M11-E3-T05 [REL]** Release stable Year 2 with QA, sample-bounded production/cost evidence, known limits, offline archive, and M12/M13 handoff.
- [ ] **M11-E3-T06 [SPEC]** Obtain M12/M13 owner acceptance of the prerequisite profile, supported assumptions, residual gaps, platform/process variants, and remediation interfaces.

## Hands-on and real-life safeguard

Year-2 success is not the percentage of boards that illuminate an LED. Reviewers sample traceability from contextual requirement through model, schematic, layout, code, measurement, fault, repair, and acceptance. A working board with no verified claim fails Project M4 acceptance.

## Required deliverables

- Frozen S3/S4 manifests and pilot protocol; direct outcome/G5/G6 and active-delivery evidence
- Calibrated laboratory/fixture evidence with out-of-tolerance dispositions and uncertainty impact
- Project M3/M4 verification plus independent local small-batch build/test/service and contribution/capability report
- External review/disposition, corrected QA records, stable EN/FR/offline release, and accepted M12/M13 prerequisite handoff

## Exit criteria

- [ ] A different competent team can reproduce at least one M4 and service a seeded fault.
- [ ] Every learner has a recorded G5/G6 pass or active, time-bounded remediation/progression hold; decision evidence shows reliable individual subsystem and PCB competence.
- [ ] Manufacturing delays/failures have workable teaching contingencies.
- [ ] Advanced-course authors receive a verified prerequisite profile.
- [ ] Calibration/function checks are traceable to the claim, and any out-of-tolerance condition has a documented effect on prior evidence.
