# M13 — Semester 6 Field Systems Production

**Goal:** create ESE321, ESE322, ESE323, ESE324, ESE325, PRJ302, program competence gate G8, and Student Project M6—a context-responsive field system with ethical deployment, local product/service ownership, and handover.

**Indicative duration:** 9–12 months; partner development starts earlier  
**Primary owners:** DSP/control/power/Linux/IC/assurance faculty and fieldwork lead  
**Depends on:** M01, M11–M12  
**Authoring entry:** approved M01 context/deployment profile, M11 prerequisite profile, and M12 S5/S6 system contract\
**Release gate:** applicable M11/M12 findings, staff field pilot, and current partner/jurisdiction approvals are dispositioned\
**Exit unlocks:** M14 and capstone-quality field practice

## Hands-on and real-life safeguard

Field need drives architecture. Cloud connectivity, AI, FPGA, or Linux is included only when it improves a measurable requirement over a simpler option. Students must compare lab and field behavior and investigate at least one mismatch. A deployed device without a willing maintainer and exit plan is a failed design.

## Required deliverables

- Approved S6/field governance contract plus six complete course/unit manifests, paired packages, claims, and qualified apparatus/field paths
- G8 examiner/remediation pack and separate ethical/partner/institutional approval records
- Project M6 context trace, staff/student field evidence, approved V2 or V3 local build/test/service/contribution dossier with sample-bounded claims, multilingual/pictorial user handover
- Signed pilot-ready EN/FR/offline teaching and governed field release

## Exit criteria

- [ ] No learner conducts stakeholder research/interviews, collects personal data, accesses a site, or deploys before a recorded G8 pass; non-passers have a remediation/progression hold and safe staff-approved work.
- [ ] Staff pilot demonstrates safe failure, rollback, offline operation, and partner value.
- [ ] M6 has stakeholder, technical, environmental, security, service, and field evidence.
- [ ] Partners accept handover or documented removal; no abandoned device/data obligation remains.
- [ ] Every learner who receives field/research access has a recorded G8 pass; sampling is used only to moderate the gate system.
