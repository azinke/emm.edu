# M14 — Year-3 Pilot and 180-Credit Exit Validation

**Goal:** validate advanced integration, field practice, the complete G1–G8 and Student Project M1–M6 record, and an optional 180-credit exit-evidence profile through per-student decisions plus external moderation; call it a *Licence* only where locally approved.

**Indicative duration:** one academic year plus correction window  
**Primary owners:** Year-3 lead, fieldwork/safety/data leads, external technical panel  
**Depends on:** M12–M13  
**Authoring entry:** M12 and M13 pilot-ready cohort baselines pass their release gates\
**Release gate:** S5/S6 delivery, correction/re-pilot, complete exit-evidence audit, and external moderation are complete\
**Exit unlocks:** reliable capstone entry and locally approved exit evidence

## Epic E1 — Readiness and controlled deployment

- [ ] **M14-E1-T01 [REV]** Audit tool images, named-jurisdiction RF authorization/limits, partner consent, staff field pilots, security boundaries, equipment, travel, and incident routes.
- [ ] **M14-E1-T02 [TEST]** Dry-run G7/G8, update/recovery, FPGA timing, Linux image, field rollback, and partner handover.
- [ ] **M14-E1-T03 [SPEC]** Freeze cohort releases, project scopes, protected data repositories, and designated sacrificial/reusable hardware.
- [ ] **M14-E1-T04 [REV]** Audit every Year-3 course manifest/unit DoD, active-learning delivery, EN/FR/accessibility/offline path, hardware/equipment path, one-term-early staff pilot, and open defect.

## Epic E2 — Delivery and direct evidence

- [ ] **M14-E2-T01 [TEST]** Sample individual RTOS, RISC-V architecture/RTL and FPGA implementation, mixed-signal, RF, reliability, DSP, power/control, Linux, IC, and assurance competence.
- [ ] **M14-E2-T02 [TEST]** Observe whether tools reveal mechanisms or become black boxes; require trace/waveform/resource/timing evidence.
- [ ] **M14-E2-T03 [TEST]** Track field system uptime, false/missed events, calibration drift, energy, connectivity, environment, maintenance, user acceptance, and incidents.
- [ ] **M14-E2-T04 [TEST]** Audit EN/FR technical-edition parity separately from partner communication in the partner's preferred language or accessible non-text route; also audit service documentation, individual roles, raw data, repository trace, and honest limitations.
- [ ] **M14-E2-T05 [TEST]** Sample an independent local design–assemble–test–service reproduction, small-batch process, and contribution/provenance record; compare only the claimed, sample-bounded V3-readiness capability with observed process evidence and do not infer an individual's contribution from a cohort sample.

## Epic E3 — Exit-level and external review

- [ ] **M14-E3-T01 [SPEC]** Map the separately authorized 180-credit derived profile to all of its declared G1–G8, Student Projects M1–M6, retained PLOs, professional portfolio, and local general-education/internship/award criteria. For every learner, record individual V2 product-design/verification evidence and an attributable, defended contribution to a V3-readiness process; record per-student completeness rather than inferring it from team or sample evidence.
- [ ] **M14-E3-T02 [REV]** External panel samples calculations, firmware, HDL timing, PCB/product evidence, field claims, safety/security, and oral defense.
- [ ] **M14-E3-T03 [TEST]** Run a cross-domain unseen practical: diagnose a networked mixed hardware/software fault and justify next action.
- [ ] **M14-E3-T04 [REV]** Analyze attainment, language, workload, partner, field, safety, and technology-access evidence.
- [ ] **M14-E3-T05 [DEC]** Clear each candidate individually only when the applicable gates, retained outcomes, individual V2 evidence, and attributable defended V3-readiness contribution are complete; otherwise record a specific remediation/progression hold. External sampling moderates decision quality and never supplies missing student evidence.

## Epic E4 — Correct and release

- [ ] **M14-E4-T01 [MAT]** Correct Year-3 materials, images, fixtures, scopes, professor notes, field protocols, and assessments in both languages.
- [ ] **M14-E4-T02 [TEST]** Re-pilot every materially changed prerequisite, safety/security/field, assessment, accessibility, language, hardware, or local-process activity and remediate individual gate gaps.
- [ ] **M14-E4-T03 [REL]** Tag stable Year-3 release and optional 180-credit exit-evidence pack with limitations and local award approval status; never imply ECTS/credit count alone grants equivalence.
- [ ] **M14-E4-T04 [SPEC]** Publish capstone-entry profiles, unresolved skill gaps, and M15 recommendations.

## Hands-on and real-life safeguard

External review follows at least two claims from requirement to field evidence and asks a student to reproduce or diagnose behavior live. Polished reports cannot compensate for missing raw measurements, timing analysis, field discrepancy, recovery, or individual understanding.

## Required deliverables

- Year-3 pilot protocol, complete course/unit/active-delivery and equipment-path audit, direct outcome plus G1–G8/M1–M6 evidence
- Field/partner/assurance report and independent local reproduce/service/contribution audit
- Per-student 180-credit exit-evidence decision, external moderation/disposition, remediation record
- Corrected stable EN/FR/offline Year-3 release and clearly labeled locally approved award or non-award exit pack

## Exit criteria

- [ ] G1–G8, Student Projects M1–M6, retained PLOs, and the professional portfolio provide credible individual and integrated exit evidence.
- [ ] Field partners confirm value, limitations, maintenance, and closure.
- [ ] A *Licence* or other award is locally approved; otherwise the artifact is clearly labeled non-award 180-credit exit evidence.
- [ ] S7/capstone authors receive a verified readiness profile.
