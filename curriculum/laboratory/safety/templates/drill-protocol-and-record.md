# ESE-SAF-FRM-004 — Emergency drill protocol and record / Protocole et fiche d'exercice d'urgence

**Template only—no drill represented / Modèle seul—aucun exercice constaté**

Select one announced, controlled simulation. Do not create real smoke, fire, exposed
energy, damaged batteries, chemical release or panic. Simulation props are clearly
marked, function-checked and energy-free. Real alarms/emergency services are notified
or isolated only through the approved local plan. A controller may stop the exercise
at any time; a real emergency cancels the simulation and takes priority.

Choisissez une simulation annoncée et maîtrisée. Ne créez ni fumée, feu, énergie
exposée, batterie réellement endommagée, rejet chimique ni panique. Les accessoires
sont marqués, vérifiés et hors énergie. Alarmes et secours ne sont informés/isolés que
selon le plan local. Le contrôleur peut arrêter ; une urgence réelle annule l'exercice.

## Scenario A — incident response / Réponse à incident

Inject: a marked low-energy apparatus shows a simulated unexpected current/odor and
a role-player reports a simulated minor injury. Expected sequence: anyone calls
`STOP / ARRÊT`; hands withdraw; designated controller isolates the simulated source;
alarm/contact and safe first-aid limits are stated; bystanders clear the route;
people are accounted for; equipment is tagged; facts and near-miss/incident route are
identified; no one resets or blames. / Injectez une anomalie simulée sur appareil marqué
et une blessure mineure jouée. Attendez arrêt, retrait, isolement simulé, alerte/secours
dans les compétences, chemin dégagé, comptage, étiquette, signalement, sans réarmement ni blâme.

## Scenario B — damaged battery/equipment quarantine / Quarantaine batterie-appareil

Inject: an **energy-free training dummy/photo card** represents a swollen battery or
damaged lead. Expected sequence: stop and establish distance; identify signs without
handling; prevent charge/use/transport; notify controller; select the approved route
and compatible simulated container; separate terminals only if the scripted safe method
allows; label identity/energy state/access; update inventory/quarantine record; state
who alone may release and what would trigger evacuation/fire response. / Une maquette
hors énergie ou carte-photo représente une batterie gonflée/un cordon endommagé.
Attendez distance, identification sans manipulation, interdiction, alerte, voie/récipient
simulés approuvés, étiquetage, registre, autorité de sortie et critères d'évacuation.

## Hot wash and corrective action / Retour immédiat et actions

Ask each role what they observed, decided and could not access. Record confusion,
unsafe simulation effects, language/access barriers, missing equipment, timing and
procedure mismatch. Do not write names in the learning summary unless required by
the protected record policy. / Demandez observations, décisions et obstacles. Consignez
confusion, effet dangereux, barrière de langue/accès, manque, temps et écart de procédure.
