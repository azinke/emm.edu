# Laboratories

Laboratories provide physical evidence for the book. They are indexed by stable
lab ID in [`catalog.toml`](catalog.toml); full lab handouts will be added only
after the apparatus and safety route has been tested.

Every lab must state the engineering question, hazard level, prerequisites,
stop conditions, circuit and test points, individual predictions, raw-data
requirements, uncertainty, a safe fault or anomaly, and the decision that the
evidence supports.

Safety levels are curriculum labels, not legal authorizations:

- `L0`: de-energized inspection or prepared data;
- `L1`: current-limited extra-low-voltage bench work;
- `L2`: supervised stored-energy, thermal, motion, soldering, or RF work;
- `L3`: institution-controlled higher-energy or regulated work;
- `L4`: specialist facility only and outside ordinary student labs.
