## 8. Defect register

Severity:

- **critical:** invalid/pressured consent; unsafe action; unapproved person/data/recording/transfer; rights or stop route fails;
- **major:** material misunderstanding, inaccessible required route, excessive burden, re-identification/confidentiality weakness, EN/FR mismatch;
- **minor:** clarity/layout issue unlikely to change choice, safety, privacy, or inclusion;
- **observation:** improvement with no current acceptance impact.
