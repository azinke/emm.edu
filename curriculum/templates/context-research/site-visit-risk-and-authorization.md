## 6. Emergency, incident, and stop-work brief

### Immediate priority

Follow the site emergency controller for evacuation, isolation, first aid, fire, security, and emergency services. Do not continue research, retrieve equipment, or protect data at the expense of people.

### Stop phrases/routes

**English:** stop / pause.
**French:** arrêtez / pause.
**Additional agreed language/gesture:** [TBD].

Anyone can stop. Mandatory stops include absent/withdrawn consent, unapproved person/data/recording, uncontrolled energy or missing guard/PPE/escort, illness/distress/harassment, changed conditions, host instruction, missed communication, device/paper loss, or a method outside approval.

### Notification order

1. site emergency/host authority for immediate physical danger;
2. institutional field-safety/travel authority;
3. research lead and independent ethics/safeguarding contact;
4. data custodian/controller for any confidentiality or device event;
5. other regulator/partner/participant notifications only through the authorized decision route.
