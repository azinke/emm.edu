## Make/buy and capability plan

For every significant make/buy choice record capability, quality, lifecycle,
lead-time, landed cost, compliance, IP/data, repair/service, alternate source,
local learning/value, and risk—not unit price alone.

## Repeat-production evidence (when V3 is claimed)

Record planned/actual sample size, traveler revision, serials, incoming inspection,
fixture function check/calibration, first-pass count/fraction, defect classes,
nonconformance dispositions, rework/scrap, labor/tooling/landed cost, independent
technician reproduction, and ambiguities corrected. Do not infer process capability
or generalized yield from an inadequate sample.
