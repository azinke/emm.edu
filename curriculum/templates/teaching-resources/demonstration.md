## Purpose and audience action

Name outcomes, prerequisite, authentic claim, prediction prompt, inclusive
response route, and the decision learners make from evidence. A demonstration is
not accepted when learners only watch.

## Setup and controls

room controls, authorized operator, safe viewing distance, stop conditions,
preflight/function check, cleanup, and reset. Identify accessible sensory routes.

## Interpretation and model limits

Compare prediction with observation, expose the intended misconception, distinguish
correlation from cause, and state what the demonstration cannot establish.

## Failure and offline fallback

Provide a tested still sequence, video/transcript, raw dataset, and analysis prompt
that preserve the reasoning construct. Prepared data may teach analysis but cannot
certify personal physical instrumentation competence.

technical/safety review, EN/FR wording review, and revisions.
