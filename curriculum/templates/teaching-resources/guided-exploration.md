# Exploration: [engineering question]

## Objective, outcome, and why it matters

State prerequisites, duration, grouping/individual evidence, context requirement,
and the claim this exploration can support.

## Equipment, substitutions, and safety

Define minimal/standard/advanced paths, setup diagram/test points, component limits,
PPE/authorization, stop conditions, accessibility routes, and approved substitute
effects.

## Predict before acting

Require a direction/order-of-magnitude/sketch plus reasoning and confidence. Do not
reveal the expected observation in the student pre-lab view when that defeats the
prediction.

## Analyze and extend

Investigate the named anomaly with competing hypotheses and a discriminating test.
Provide one-variable extension and one open design extension with an acceptance
test and a decision justified by evidence.

## Evidence to submit and exit check

enrichment route.

## Instructor annex (controlled view)

Record expected ranges (not fictitious exact values), likely substitute effects,
representative data, misconceptions, safe recovery, timing, and pilot evidence.
