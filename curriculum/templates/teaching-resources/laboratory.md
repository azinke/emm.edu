Compare prediction ↔ model/simulation ↔ measurement. Learners retain anomalies,

## Instructor annex (controlled view)

preparation/reset/consumables; seeded-fault insertion/recovery; diagnostic tree;
substitute effects; accessibility adaptations; demo/data fallback; pilot records
for every declared path; and known limitations. Prepared data cannot certify a
personal physical competence.
