# [Course] prerequisite diagnostic

## Learner information

Explain that this check selects support and does not lower outcomes or exclude a
learner. State duration, allowed tools, accessible formats, language choice, data
use/retention, and feedback route.

## Construct map

Sample algebra/graphs/units, relevant science, technical reading, digital/tool
literacy, and safety only where required by the course. Do not use background
knowledge unrelated to the construct.

## Learner items

For each item provide a stable ID, prompt, response format, time estimate,
accessible stimulus description, and confidence prompt. Use plausible mistakes to
diagnose reasoning, not trick wording.

## Scoring and routing annex (staff)

For each response pattern record the correct reasoning, misconception tag,
severity (refresh / prerequisite gap / safety hold), feedback text, targeted
resource, practice task, recheck method, and escalation owner. A course aggregate
never substitutes for a named safety or progression gate.

## Parity and QA

Record construct/parity review, answer and numerical equivalence, pilot response
time by language, accessibility check, defective-item disposition, and version.
